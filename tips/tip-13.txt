TITLE	Remove fan-outs with triggerize
DETAIL	You can get rid of fan-outs (where a single message outlet is connected to multiple inlets) by selecting objects that have fan-outs and then 'triggerizing' the selection with Ctrl-T.
DETAIL	
DETAIL	You can also select all objects (Ctrl-A) and then triggerize the entire window in a single go.
